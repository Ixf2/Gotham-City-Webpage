Gotham City Map

Gotham City is a place where light constantly struggles against darkness. Founded as a thriving urban center, it gradually became a hotbed of corruption and crime, ruled by both corrupt politicians and street violence. Its gothic skyscrapers and shadowy alleys reflect the city’s duality: wealth and decay, power and despair.

It is home to heroes and villains alike: Batman patrols its rooftops, facing criminals like the Joker, the Penguin, and Two-Face, while the city struggles to hold on to hope. Every corner of Gotham tells stories of mystery, intrigue, and tragedy, making it more than just a setting—it is a living character within the Batman universe.
